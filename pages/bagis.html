How We Use Your Donations?

Save Earth is the only global environmental charity that accepts no corporate or government donations so we can maintain a much needed independent voice, but it means we rely on individual supporters like you to fund our work.


By making a much needed donation you will join "Save Earth" in our work to protect our precious planet and find the solutions we need to our most important environmental issues.


You are helping Save Earth investigate, campaign, document and lobby for a sustainable balance between humans and the environment.
